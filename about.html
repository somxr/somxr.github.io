BIO

I am a digital creator and an aspiring designer in mixed reality (XR), which is an umbrella term for augmented + virtual reality (AR+VR). I'm based in Montreal, Canada, and I graduated from Concordia University's computer science program in 2022, with a concentration in computer games. Currently, I develop augmented reality experiences in the form of Instagram and TikTok effects. I find social AR to be among the most versatile forms of self-expression as well as marketing tools in modern technology.
