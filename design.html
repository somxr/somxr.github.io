Spirit Tales

Board Game Design

Every round, all players are "Pure", except one "Possessed" player. The Possessed card has a "Ritual Word" that the spirit must include in their story as part of a spell to stay connected to the mortal word. The Ritual Words are designed to force the Possessed player to fabricate a story. I worked on designing the game mechanics, word selection, and brand identity of the game alongside my team. My teammates created the illustrations and cards. The photos shown were shot by me.
